History and Leadership

Founded in 1979 by Donald Ferguson, Land Dynamics, Inc., quickly became known for quality General Office and Medical Office properties. While new construction of buildings were the foundation of their business offering, Land Dynamics gained a reputation for upgrading existing unique buildings and creating value through a close, hands-on management style and a commitment to full occupancy leasing with quality tenants.

1000 Des Peres Road Des Peres, MO 63131

• Principal owner & manager
• Des Peres, MO, 63131
• Acquired: February 2005
• Square Feet: 75,000

Land Dynamics purchased this property in February 2005, located across the street from the City of Des Peres $38 million recreation facility. The building is 75,000 square feet consisting of multi tenant medical practices. Rehabbing of the building consists of resurfacing the parking areas, expanding the parking lot and interior lobby renovations. This project was completed in November 2005.

7710-7730 Carondelet Avenue Clayton, MO 63105

• Principal owner & manager
• Coveted Clayton Business District
• Acquired: August 2007
• Multiple Tenants (Office, Legal, Admin)
• Square Feet: 90,000 (combined)

Land Dynamics not only looks for the single-use facilities, we know how to put you in a multi-use facility in the heart of the action. This property consists of two office buildings that share a common lobby. Carondelet East (7710) building offers 52,350 rentable square feet while Carondelet West (7730) offers of 37,121 rentable square feet. The buildings have a parking structure attached with approximately 300 spaces with 2 levels of covered parking—a premium in this desirable location.

12680 Olive Creve Coeur, MO 63141

• Principal owner & manager
• Mixed-use Medical
• Acquired: March 2002
• Square Feet: 37,500

Land Dynamics sees more to a building than it’s current façade. Originally a Firstar Bank Office building, we saw an ideal facility to link to and support St. John’s Mercy medical offices. Located at the corner of Olive and Mason Roads, adjacent to BJC West County Hospital, the building originally consisted of 20,000 square feet and was expanded to 37,500. It features a covered drop-off area for easy access for patients.
