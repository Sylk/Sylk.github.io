Marita's Lyrics

Hello, my name is Marita Sykes Ray, a songwriter in St. Louis, Mo. In January 2013, I signed a three year contract with Justin Peters of Platinum Planet Music, Inc., a production company in Nashville, Tenn. My mission is to have my songs performed and recorded on a national and international level. My genres include Pop, R&B, and Country, Gospel and holiday music. I might be a little all over the place, but I write how and what I am feeling at the moment. I believe everyone is born with a gift or talent; the trick is to recognize you have it. My passion and gift is songwriting. Setting short stories to melodies is relaxing, enjoying and soothing to me. I hope you like what you hear. Visit my site often; I am constantly adding additional songs for you to hear and maybe even buy. Thanks for stopping by!
